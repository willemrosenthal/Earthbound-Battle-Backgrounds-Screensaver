A native macOS screensaver of the EarthBound / Mother 2 battle backgrounds.
Universal build — runs on Apple Silicon and Intel, macOS 12 or later.

## What's new in v1.1.0

- **Choose your backgrounds** — the Options sheet now shows an animated preview of
  every background; uncheck any you don't want in the random rotation (with
  **Enable All** / **Disable All** shortcuts).
- **Click any preview** to see that background full screen, just like the real
  screensaver (Esc or click to dismiss).
- **Color saturation** slider — make the colors as vivid as you like.
- **Single-background chance** — control how often the screensaver shows a single
  background versus two blended together.
- All-black "empty layer" backgrounds are now hidden from the picker and never
  chosen, so you'll never land on a black screen.

## Install

1. Download **EarthboundBattle-Screensaver.zip** below and unzip it.
2. Right-click (Control-click) **Install.command** and choose **Open**
   (macOS will warn it's from an unidentified developer — click Open).
3. Open **System Settings → Screen Saver** and pick **EarthboundBattle**.

See the included **README.txt** for a manual install option and the **Options…**
settings.

This is a free, fan-made project and is not notarized by Apple, which is why the
one-time "unidentified developer" prompt appears. It is safe to open.
